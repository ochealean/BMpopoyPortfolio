About BM Popoy

A Leader Rooted in Service

Hon. Romano "Popoy" L. Del Rosario is a Provincial Board Member representing the 3rd District of Bataan. A dedicated public servant, he has devoted his career to uplifting communities and championing policies that drive meaningful progress. Known for his hands-on approach and genuine compassion for the people he serves, BM Popoy has become a trusted voice in local governance.

With a deep understanding of the challenges facing ordinary citizens, he consistently advocates for transparency, accountability, and inclusive development ensuring that no community is left behind.
