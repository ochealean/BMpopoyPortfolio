About BM Popoy

A Leader Rooted in Service

Hon. Romano "Popoy" L. Del Rosario is a Provincial Board Member representing the 3rd District of Bataan. A dedicated public servant, he has devoted his career to uplifting communities and championing policies that drive meaningful progress. Known for his hands-on approach and genuine compassion for the people he serves, BM Popoy has become a trusted voice in local governance.

With a deep understanding of the challenges facing ordinary citizens, he consistently advocates for transparency, accountability, and inclusive development ensuring that no community is left behind.

Vision

A progressive, empowered, and united community where every citizen has equal access to opportunities, quality public services, and a dignified life.

Mission

To serve the people with integrity, champion legislation that benefits the marginalized, and build bridges between government and the communities it serves.

What I Stand For

Key Advocacies

Education

Ensuring quality and accessible education for all from scholarships to improved school facilities and teacher support programs.

Health & Wellness

Promoting comprehensive healthcare access, medical missions, and mental health awareness across all communities.

Environment

Championing sustainable development, clean-up drives, tree-planting programs, and environmental protection ordinances.

Social Welfare

Supporting livelihood programs, disaster relief, and social safety nets for the vulnerable and marginalized sectors.

Infrastructure

Pushing for modern infrastructure better roads, bridges, public buildings, and connectivity to drive economic growth.

Youth Empowerment

Investing in the youth through training, sports programs, and leadership development to build the next generation of leaders.
